Ingredients

  • 32 oz of plain greek yogurt
  • 18 oz of creamy peanut butter
  • 1-2 mashed bananas or cubed apples

Preparation

  1. Mix the ingredients together.
  2. Pour into a paper cup or silicone mold.
  3. Freeze overnight.
  4. Top with whipped cream before serving!
